About Me

I'm Josue Martinez-Martinez, an engineer, AI researcher, and recent PhD graduate in Computer Science & Engineering from the University of Connecticut. I was supervised by Dr. Sheida Nabavi and Dr. Derek Aguiar and conducted my doctoral research in collaboration with MIT Lincoln Laboratory, where I was mentored by Olivia Brown. My research focuses on building machine learning systems that remain reliable under distribution shift and adversarial stress, with interests spanning synthetic media detection, model reliability, and self-supervised learning. My work has been supported by competitive fellowships and grants from NSF, NASA, GEM, General Electric, and the University of Connecticut.

I'm currently seeking research positions and collaboration opportunities in AI assurance and trustworthy AI systems.
